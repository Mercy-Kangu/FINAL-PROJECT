Tree planting Projects

We not only manage our waste but also bring forth more life by planting trees and we give a bright smile while at it

You are wondering how?

We got you! We are going to learn the major steps for planting a tree

  1. Pick a Spot: Choose a sunny place with space for roots to grow.
  2. Dig a Hole: Make it twice as wide as the tree's root ball.
  3. Place the Tree: Gently lower the tree into the hole, keeping it straight.
  4. Cover & Water: Fill the hole with soil, pat it down, and water it well!

Bonus: Give your tree a name and take care of it every week!
